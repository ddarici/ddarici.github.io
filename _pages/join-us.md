We are always looking for motivated and talented individuals at each career stage to join our team.

## What we look for
- Strong background in medicine, cognitive neuroscience, or computational biology.
- Passion for educational research and solving computational problems
- Ability to work independently and collaboratively
- Good written and verbal communication skills in English
- Prior research experience is a plus
- Programming skills (R, Python) are a plus
- Experience with Eye-tracking, EEG, or other biosensors is a plus

## How to Apply
Send your CV, a brief research statement (1 page max.), and contact details to the email below.
<br> <br>
Once we receive your application, we will be in touch to discuss how we can best support you, whether that means finding the right project fit within the group, supervising a thesis, connecting you with suitable collaborators, or exploring funding opportunities such as doctoral scholarships or Postdoctoral fellowships together. We also warmly encourage undergraduate students to apply and explore opportunities to get involved in our research.
